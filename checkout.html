2. Pricing model

Do not force full 7TRB for everything. Start with three pricing lanes and let the product decide the payment mix.

Recommended rules

  • Small items under $35: allow up to 100% 7TRB for promos
  • Mid-range items $35–$75: use hybrid pricing like cash + 7TRB
  • Bigger bundles: use custom split or limited special drops
