Exit Options

How to Sell or Redeem 7TRB

1. Exchange / P2P

Sell on JollofSwap DEX or peer-to-peer with other community members.

2. Buyback Requests

Request buyback from treasury (depends on liquidity and rules).

3. Unstaking

Unstake at preset cycles once staking rules are active.

Important: The goal of 7TRB is circulation, not speculation. Redemption is rule-based to protect the treasury and encourage long-term participation.
