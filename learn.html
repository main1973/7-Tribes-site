Crypto 101 — Doubts & Answers

1) What makes a coin go up or down?
  • Short answer: supply + demand.
  • More buyers than sellers → price up. More sellers than buyers → price down.
  • Also matters: utility (can you use it), trust (transparent devs/treasury), scarcity (fixed supply), and news/hype/fear.
2) What if a billionaire buys it all?
  • That’s a whale attack. We counter with: wide distribution, community treasury + voting, liquidity pools, and social defense (in extremes, communities can fork).
3) What if they dump it cheap?
  • That’s price manipulation. Thin markets are fragile. We counter with real usage (merchants + projects), lots of holders (dilution of whale power), and radical transparency (live dashboard; receipts over rhetoric).
Street version: 1 guy owning 10 houses can play landlord. 10,000 families owning their homes? No single landlord can crash it. Same idea here.

Common Myths (Debunked)

“Crypto is all scams.”

7TRB ties value to work you can verify: merchants, funded projects, and a public dashboard. If you can’t verify it, don’t trust it.

“If I lose my phone, I lose my crypto.”

Your wallet is recovered with your seed phrase. Store it offline. Phone lost ≠ coins lost.

“It’s anonymous crime money.”

Blockchains are public ledgers. Transactions are traceable. Good opsec matters, but it’s not a shadow realm.

“Crypto is illegal.”

Laws vary by country, but owning/using crypto is legal in most places. We don’t give legal/tax advice—know your local rules.

Safety & Wallet Security

Seed Phrase Rules

  • Write it on paper. Store offline. Never screenshot.
  • Never share it. No admin or “support” needs it. Ever.
  • Consider two copies in separate safe spots.

Device Hygiene

  • Update your OS & browser.
  • Use a password manager + 2FA.
  • Bookmark official links (this site, Etherscan contract page).

Scam Radar

  • Too-good-to-be-true “airdrops”.
  • DMs offering help. Fake support.
  • Clone websites (check URL carefully).

Glossary (Plain Language)

Wallet — An app that holds your keys. It’s your account. Protect your seed phrase.

Gas — The small ETH fee to run a transaction on Ethereum.

DEX / CEX — Decentralized or centralized exchange where you trade coins.

Liquidity — How easy it is to buy/sell without moving price.

Treasury — Community funds controlled by proposals/votes.

DAO — Community governance: proposals + votes = action.
