How Onboarding Works

The goal is simple: join the network, create your identity, and start building connection, support, and revenue inside the ecosystem.

Step 1 — Choose your role Join as a resident, creator, or business. Your role shapes how your profile appears inside the network.
Step 2 — Build your profile Add your name, image, city, bio, services, links, and anything people need to know to connect with you.
Step 3 — Get listed Once added, your profile becomes part of the 7TRB network directory where people can discover and support you.
Step 4 — Circulate Promote your offers, connect with others, accept support, and become part of a living economic network.
