About 7Tribes (7TRB)

7Tribes is a community-powered movement built on unity, innovation, and economic empowerment. Success depends on participation—builders, investors, and supporters circulating value inside the community.

Why We Need People for 7Tribes to Succeed

Ownership = responsibility. Spend some to power today. Hold some to build tomorrow. Teach one, bring two.

Investment

Policy: Spend 30% to power projects and merchants; hold 70% for long-term community wealth.

Participate

  • Circulate: buy from Tribe merchants, fund projects.
  • Contribute: share, teach, onboard.
  • Create: propose builds, volunteer skills.
  • Govern: read proposals, vote, track funds.

Roadmap (First 90 Days)

  • Day 1–30: 500 holders, 5 merchants, 1 pilot project.
  • Day 31–60: 1,000 holders, 15 merchants, 3 projects.
  • Day 61–90: 2,000 holders, 30 merchants, 6 projects.
