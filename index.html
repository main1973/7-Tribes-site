Why 7TRB Exists

Most communities earn money and then watch it leave. Rent, taxes, outside businesses, and disconnected spending pull value out faster than people can rebuild it.

7TRB exists to test a different model: organize people, connect businesses, and keep value circulating between the people creating it.

Products, tools, and education support that mission — they are not the mission.

This model is being explored locally first, with connected participation from Africa and other communities over time.

How 7TRB Works

7TRB is not just a token — it is a system designed to keep value circulating inside the community.

1. Organize

Bring people together — residents, businesses, and creators — into one connected network.

2. Build

Create products, services, and opportunities inside the network. Support each other first.

3. Circulate

Spend within the system. Money stays moving — and movement creates value.
