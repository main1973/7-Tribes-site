AmVault Treasury & Wallet Transparency

Trust gets stronger when movement is visible. 7TRB is being structured around wallet identity, transparent treasury flow, and wallet-to-wallet participation through AmVault.

Register

Residents and merchants register through AmVault so participation begins with a visible wallet identity layer.

Transact

7TRB moves wallet to wallet, creating a transparent record of activity across merchants, residents, and treasury flow.

Verify

Treasury reporting, circulation metrics, and reward activity can be measured from actual movement instead of promises.

Treasury Structure

  • AmVault treasury wallet acts as the main reporting layer
  • Rewards and distribution can be tracked from a defined source
  • Wallet-to-wallet movement helps strengthen accountability

Why It Matters
