About 7Tribes (7TRB)

7Tribes isn't just a token — it's a blueprint for independence. The mission is simple: redirect the flow of money back into the people's hands. Instead of watching wealth leak out into banks, corporations, and gatekeepers, 7TRB keeps value moving inside the community that built it.

The People’s Currency for a New Era

7TRB is more than a token — it’s a movement. In a world where the U.S. dollar runs on trust in governments, and Bitcoin runs on trust in code, 7TRB runs on trust in people.

This is currency built from community, fueled by culture, and strengthened by real-world circulation.

While old financial systems argue over power, and the digital gold rush chases speculation, 7TRB grows from something deeper: unity, usage, and ownership.

No institutions. No gatekeepers. No middlemen. Just a tribe committed to building its own value — one wallet, one product, one connection at a time.

Why the Name "7Tribes"?

The number 7 represents completion, unity, and power across cultures. Tribes are about people — families, builders, hustlers, farmers, creators — moving as one. 7Tribes = many individuals forming one unstoppable collective.

Why People Matter

A token without people is just code on a blockchain. 7Tribes only works if people join, participate, and take responsibility.

  • Holding some 7TRB for the future.
  • Spending some with merchants to grow the local economy.
  • Bringing in new voices — every sign-up strengthens the network.
  • Voting on projects and proposals that matter most.

Together, that unlocks what 7Tribes can become — a living network where every wallet, trade, and purchase builds real community wealth.

A Practical Guide for Tribes, Builders, Merchants & the 7Tribes Economy

SECTION 1 — What 7TRB Is

7TRB is a community-powered currency designed to circulate wealth inside tribes. It funds builders, supports merchants, and strengthens local economies through transparency and trust.

SECTION 2 — Building Your Tribe

Step 1 — Gather Your People

  • Start with 5–20 committed members.
  • Create a group on Telegram, WhatsApp, Discord, or Facebook.
  • Choose a mission and a tribe name.
SECTION 3 — Leadership Structure
  • Coordinator: runs meetings and communication.
  • Treasurer: manages wallet and treasury tracking.
  • Recorder: handles votes, notes, and updates.
SECTION 4 — Builders & Project Funding
  1. Create a proposal.
  2. Submit to tribe.
  3. Discuss and vote.
  4. Receive funds in USD / 7TRB.
  5. Report progress until delivered.
SECTION 5 — Merchants
  • Create wallet.
  • Submit merchant form.
  • Add 7TRB Accepted badge or sign.
  • Offer partial payments, loyalty rewards, or tribe-only discounts.
SECTION 6 — Dashboard Guide
  • Holders: community size.
  • Active Wallets: movement.
  • Treasury: community war chest.
  • Merchants: adoption strength.
