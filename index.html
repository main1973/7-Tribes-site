What is 7 Tribes?

7 Tribes is more than a coin — it's a digital liberation movement for the Black community worldwide. We are reconnecting our people through culture, technology, and economic power. Whether in Africa, the Caribbean, Europe, or the Americas — we are one Tribe with one goal: collective wealth and freedom.
