Loop — Community & Governance

What is Loop?

Loop is the heartbeat of 7Tribes. It's where members gather, discuss ideas, vote on proposals, and track community progress. Loop is the town hall, the voting floor, and the activity feed — all in one.

Key Features

  • Town Hall: Weekly/bi-weekly community calls and updates.
  • Proposals & Voting: Members propose projects, vote on funding, and track delivery.
  • Activity Feed: Real-time updates on new members, referrals, and ecosystem activity.
  • Transparency: All treasury movements and decisions logged on-chain.
  • Rewards: Participation badges, referral bounties, and governance incentives.

Connect — Marketplace & Profiles

What is Connect?

Connect is the marketplace where people find each other, businesses showcase services, and value circulates. It's a network of profiles, storefronts, referrals, and real economic activity.

Key Features

  • People Profiles: Build your reputation, show your work, connect with others.
  • Business Storefronts: Merchants list services, set prices, accept 7TRB payments.
  • Referral System: Earn bounties for bringing new members and businesses.
  • Reputation & Ratings: Trust built through reviews, completed transactions, and community feedback.
  • Messaging: Direct communication between buyers, sellers, and collaborators.

Nuru — Wallet, Identity & Rewards

What is Nuru?

Nuru is your identity and wallet in the 7Tribes ecosystem. It's where you hold 7TRB, manage your reputation, access rewards, and browse the dApp ecosystem. Nuru is built on Alkebuleum and integrates with AmVault.

Key Features

  • Wallet: Hold, send, and receive 7TRB securely.
  • Identity: Your on-chain profile linked to Loop and Connect.
  • Reputation Score: Built from transactions, votes, and community participation.
  • Rewards: Earn 7TRB from referrals, governance, and ecosystem participation.
  • dApp Browser: Access JollofSwap, staking, and other 7Tribes tools.

Alkebuleum — Blockchain Infrastructure

What is Alkebuleum?

Alkebuleum is the decentralized blockchain that powers 7Tribes. It's where 7TRB lives, where transactions are recorded, and where the future of the ecosystem is built. Alkebuleum is independent, community-focused, and designed for real use.

Key Features

  • 7TRB Token: Lives on Alkebuleum Mainnet with full transparency.
  • AKE Gas Token: Powers transactions on the chain.
  • JollofSwap DEX: Trade, swap, and provide liquidity for 7TRB.
  • Smart Contracts: Treasury, governance, and future dApps run on-chain.
  • On-Chain Transparency: Every transaction, every proposal, every receipt is verifiable.
