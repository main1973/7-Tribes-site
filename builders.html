How You Get Paid

Flexible Payment Models

Milestone Payments

Get paid in stages: 30% at start, 40% at mid-point, 30% at completion. Payment follows verified progress.

7TRB + USD

Choose your mix. Some builders prefer 100% 7TRB (more upside). Others prefer 50/50 or USD-heavy.

Equity / Ownership

Long-term builders can negotiate equity stakes or treasury allocation for major contributions.

Referral Bounties

Bring in new builders, merchants, or members. Earn referral rewards in 7TRB.

Transparency: All payments are logged on-chain. Every builder knows what everyone else earned. No secrets.
