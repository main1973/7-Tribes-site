Crypto 101 — Doubts & Answers

What makes a coin go up or down?

Supply & demand. More buyers than sellers → up. More sellers than buyers → down. Utility, trust, scarcity, and news amplify moves.

Is crypto safe?

It’s digital money on a public ledger—anyone can check the receipts. But you are your own bank: protect your wallet and recovery phrase.

Isn’t it all scams?

Some coins are hype. 7TRB ties value to real use: merchants, projects, and a transparency dashboard you can verify.

What if a billionaire buys it all or dumps?

We defend with wide distribution, community treasury & votes, steady real-world use, and liquidity. Power stays with people, not whales.

Why Ethereum?

It’s the most proven smart-contract network. Yes, there are gas fees, but reliability and security matter for long-term community money.
