How 7TRB Works

This page is the 7TRB operating system. It explains how tribes use wallets, treasury discipline, and merchants to create real circulation. No hype. No promises. Structure first.

7TRB is a coordination + circulation tool inside small disciplined groups. Tokens amplify whatever system you put them inside. Discipline becomes stronger. Chaos collapses faster.

Non-negotiable rule: Structure first. Token second. Always.

What 7TRB is

A system for documenting contribution, rewarding participation, and circulating value inside tribes and merchant networks.

What 7TRB is not

Not a promise of profit. Not a substitute for leadership. Not a replacement for agreements or accountability.

The simple model

Earn → Spend → Recirculate. If 7TRB doesn’t move through real use, it becomes narrative instead of utility.

Wallets (identity + responsibility)

Member wallet: controlled by the individual. Receives 7TRB for verified contribution.

Tribe treasury wallet: owned by the tribe’s rules (not a person). Used only for approved purposes with reporting.

Merchant wallet: receives 7TRB for real goods/services. Merchants participate in circulation.

Why this matters: mixing personal and treasury funds creates “he said/she said.” Separation prevents conflict.

Treasury (purpose + discipline)

The treasury funds tribe-approved goals: projects, shared infrastructure, stability.

Non-negotiables: (1) every transaction recorded, (2) purpose-bound spending aligned to mission, (3) scheduled reporting.

Most groups don’t fail from theft — they fail from undocumented spending and unclear authority.

Tribes (the operating unit)

7TRB scales through many small tribes (3–7 people), not one massive crowd. Small groups keep effort visible and accountability real.

Merchants (utility + retention)

Merchants give 7TRB real utility by accepting it for goods/services.

Never sell merchants on “future price.” Sell them on customer flow, retention, and network spend.

Merchants operate on margins, not dreams.

Circulation (the point)

Healthy circulation: members earn for contribution → spend with merchants/tribe services → merchants recirculate/redeem by agreement.

Hoarding without purpose is usually a governance failure, not a market signal.

Start a 7TRB Tribe (minimum setup)

Before using 7TRB, your tribe must write these down:

  1. Mission — what problem you solve using 7TRB
  2. Roles — who is responsible for what
  3. Contribution rules — what counts + how verified
  4. Decision method — vote/lead/supermajority
  5. Treasury rules — who can do what + reporting cadence
  6. Conflict process — steps before it becomes personal
  7. Exit rules — leaving without destruction

Next: Once that structure exists, introduce 7TRB distributions for verified contribution.

FAQ

Do I need 7TRB to start a tribe?

No. Start with structure. Token comes after the tribe proves it can follow rules.

How do we reward contribution with 7TRB?

Only for verified, documented contribution (completed tasks, delivered outcomes, agreed participation). No emotional distributions.

What stops one person from controlling everything?

Role separation + treasury rules + scheduled reporting. If one person controls the treasury without oversight, it isn’t decentralized.
