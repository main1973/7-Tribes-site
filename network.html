Reward structure

Rewards should push behavior, not just look nice on paper. Reward the actions that keep the exchange alive.

Accept

  • First qualified 7TRB transaction: +10 7TRB
  • Three partner transactions in a month: +25 7TRB
  • Monthly active merchant badge and spotlight

Re-spend

  • Use earned 7TRB within 7 days: circulation bonus
  • Spend with two or more partner businesses: extra multiplier
  • Top circulator: spotlight and bonus reward

Refer

  • Bring in one active business: +20 7TRB
  • Bring in a business that completes 3 trades: bigger reward
  • Builder status for repeated quality referrals

Network rules

Keep the system honest. This is where a network becomes a real operating model instead of a mess.

Business requirements

  • List what you sell and what you buy
  • Set a clear percentage accepted in 7TRB
  • Accept at least some amount of 7TRB to join
  • Be reachable through approved contact information

Anti-abuse rules

  • No fake transactions to farm rewards
  • No circular spam transfers with no business purpose
  • All rewards depend on real exchange activity
  • Abuse can remove a business from the directory
